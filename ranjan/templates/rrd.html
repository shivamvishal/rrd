What We Do

Event Planning

Meetings, parties and ceremonies bring people together, and meeting and we ensure that this purpose is achieved seamlessly. We coordinate every detail of meetings and conventions, from the speakers and lightings to catering and tents we take care of everything.

Caterering

Looking for a perfect food affair to complement your special occasion?
Look no further! We provide a sumptuous food itinerary to fulfill your catering need. Be it a large, mid or small sized event, our full-range of outdoor and indoor catering services cover complete food preparation and presentation for formal and informal gatherings.

Lease

We offer a great range of tent rental service, wedding tent rental service, lighting decoration and theme party tent rental services.

About Us

We’ve been around for more than 50 years and counting. A lot has changed in all that time, we’ve grown in size and in scale. We have extended our services beyond Dhanbad to many other cites and looking forward for more. But there are a lot of things that haven’t changed, like our commitment to making our stores better every day for our Associates, customers and the community.

From 1967 to present, we have served in thousands of parties, meetings and functions ranging from birthday parties to big concerts.

Our History

We has been around for more than 50 years. We started small, back in 1967 established by Late Shri Raj Mangal Singh and Shri Nain Ranjan Das. With years of hard work and commitment towards our customer we have become a renowned decorator house.

A Legacy of Diversity

We’re here in your neighborhood, and draw upon your rich culture to make our stores better. We’ve also made it a point to ensure that our suppliers are representative of the communities we serve. We offer a diverse range of services and product to match the need of each customer.
